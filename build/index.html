What is harm reduction?

Drug harm reduction consists in education, both legal and illegal to help reduce the short and long term harms of drugs. There are very clear benefits to knowing which dose range to stick to or learning how to handle a bad trip.We do not promote drug use, but we do encourage safety for those thinking of taking any kind of drug.

We cannot guarantee that this will prevent any harm from drug use, nor can we be liable for any outcomes resulting from the use of drugs. The information must be treated as indicative only as individuals and circumstances will differ.

This website was inspired by the increasing number of deaths that occur in the UK and USA due to lack of correct drug education. If you support our work please consider donating. If you’d like to know more about where your donation will go send us an email.
