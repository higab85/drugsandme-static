Workshops and Talks on drugs

We design and facilitate bespoke workshops for a wide range of audiences on drugs and addiction across the UK. We specialise in providing clear, evidence based and effective messages based upon the key principles of quality drugs education.

Our primary aim is the prevent the harms of drugs by empowering people with the information they need to help them and those they care about to make informed choices.

University training days

We deliver comprehensive training days for universities on drugs, addiction and psychosocial interventions across the UK. Our training sessions are ideal for university courses in which drugs are a relevant issue but not to the extent where full modules are part of the programme.

We design and modify our courses on request to ensure that the content is relevant to the programme.

These sessions are delivered by our Training Director Paul North who has worked in drug treatment services and lectured at universities on drugs and addiction for nine years. He currently works for a leading drug policy think tank and can cover topics on policy and government as-well as drugs and addiction.
